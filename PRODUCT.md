# JT ALWM Hub

## Product purpose
A web hub for JT ALWM teams to collect weekly field footage and scripts, then let editors review and download by week and country.

## Users
- Correspondents: upload video, audio, and scripts quickly without training.
- Editors: review, filter, and download packs by week and country.

## Register
product

## Brand and tone
- Calm, professional, and dependable.
- Clear labels, low friction, minimal surprises.

## Core tasks
- Choose a country, upload files, and confirm delivery.
- Browse weekly uploads, preview scripts, and download a country pack.

## Non-goals
- No social or public sharing.
- No complex account management or roles yet.

## Constraints
- Must stay light and fast on modest laptops.
- Must be readable on small 13-14 inch screens.

## Success signals
- Uploads feel obvious and safe.
- Editors can find a country and download in seconds.
